Privacy Policy

This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use Classic Academy. Template text — customize for your legal requirements and your school’s data policies.

Contact Pricing

Last updated: 2026-03-03

1) Information we collect

We may collect information you provide (name, email, phone, school information) and operational data necessary to deliver the service (student records, attendance, fees records) depending on your configured modules.

2) How we use information

  • Provide and improve the platform and support
  • Enable admissions, communications, and reports requested by your school
  • Secure accounts, prevent fraud, and enforce access controls
  • Process payments and invoices where applicable

3) Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell personal data. We may share data only:

  • With service providers who help us operate the platform (hosting, SMS/email, analytics) under appropriate safeguards
  • When required by law, regulation, or valid legal process
  • With your authorized school administrators and staff, based on role permissions

4) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to minimize risk through access controls, auditing, and secure infrastructure.

5) Data retention

We retain data as long as needed to provide the service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Schools may request exports and controlled deletion according to policy and applicable law.

6) Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction and relationship (student/parent/staff/admin), you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete information. Contact us to process requests.

7) Contact

Questions? Reach us via the Contact page or call/WhatsApp using the support button.
